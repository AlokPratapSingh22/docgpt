Delete Schedule LogsThe periodic scheduling of background jobs generates many log entries which must be deleted at regular intervals.About This Task In the Schedule Logs page, you can delete logs from the Successful and Failed sections.ProcedureIn the module navigation menu, select Admin > Operations Manager > Schedule Logs.Select the Successful or Failed tab.To filter the logs that you want to delete, select the dates (that is, the period) in the boxes below the Started column heading.Select Delete.The logs are deleted.Note: The deleted logs cannot be retrieved.
