WorkflowM&D Analyst Typical WorkflowThe analyst is responsible for triaging alerts/alarms/incidents/events, resolving trips (asset outages), and diagnosing alerts/alarms/events/incidents. Quality and accuracy are critical. Typical response requirements can include:Priority Alert recommendations to the customer control room are required within 30 minutes.A resolution email to the customer is required within 45 minutes.Other alerts should be dispositioned within 23-40 minutes.Complex alarms should be resolved within 24 hours after consultation with the customer, on-site engineers, and other appropriate resources to determine the root cause.Figure: M&D Analyst Typical Workflow
