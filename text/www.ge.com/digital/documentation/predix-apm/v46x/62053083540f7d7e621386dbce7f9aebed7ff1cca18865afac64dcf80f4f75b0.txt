Third Quarter of 2020Release Date: August 14, 2020This topic provides a list of product changes released for this module on this date.
Table 1. Resolved Issues
The following issues, which existed in one or more previous versions, have been resolved.
DescriptionTracking IDPreviously, when measurement data was received for multiple assets from a datalogger, and you saved the TML records to GE Digital APM, the analyses were not calculated for all the assets. This issue has been resolved. Now, in this scenario, the save and calculate jobs have been moved to scheduler, and the analyses are re-calculated for all the assets.DE137494
