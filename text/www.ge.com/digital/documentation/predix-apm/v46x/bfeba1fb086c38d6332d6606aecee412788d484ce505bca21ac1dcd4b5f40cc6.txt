Add a Field to a Group of FieldsProcedure
Open the datasheet containing the group that you want to modify.In the Available Items pane, select the item that you want to add to the group, and then drag it into the group.The item is added to the group.Note: The newly added field will be available for you in Family Management, whereas to access the field from other modules, you must log out and log in to GE Digital APM. For other users who are being logged in to GE Digital APM, the created field will be available only when they log in to the application again.
