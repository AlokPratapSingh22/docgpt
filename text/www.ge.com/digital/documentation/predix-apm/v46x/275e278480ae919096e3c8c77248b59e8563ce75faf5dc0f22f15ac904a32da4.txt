Second Quarter of 2020Release Date: May 8, 2020This topic provides a list of product changes released for this module on this date.
Table 1. Enhancements and New Features
The following enhancements and new features have been added.
DescriptionTracking IDNew content is added to Compliance Management to support the European Inspection requirements. European Inspection Management provides a solution to categorize the inspection activities according to government regulation, based on the pressure, volume, and fluid characteristics. According to this categorization, the Compliance Management module of GE Digital APM can recommend applicable inspection task types along with a recommended interval based on each asset type. It also helps you to identify the certification requirements of Inspectors, such as ZÜS (Approved Inspection Body) or bP (Qualified Person), based on the criticality of the asset.This functionality is fully integrated with the Compliance Management module and the Inspection Management module.F39687
