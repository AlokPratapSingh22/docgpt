Remove a Field from a GroupProcedure
Open the datasheet containing the field group that you want to modify.Select the item that you want to remove from the group and drag it to the Available Items pane.The item is removed from the group.Note: The field that is removed will no longer be available for you in Family Management, whereas to ensure that the field is not accessible from other modules, you must log out and log in to GE Digital APM. For other users who are being logged in to GE Digital APM, the deleted field will not be available only when they log in to the application again.
